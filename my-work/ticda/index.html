My very first sponsored project! The first part of the shoot is heavily inspired from product photography pages I see on Instagram with incredible setups so I attempted a DIY shoot with fake grass from my dad’s koi pond and his fishing line to suspend the armband! A few sprinkles of water later I got the shot and I guess that day ‘I could do anything’ (TICDA hehe)

The second part of the shoot was an outdoor shoot since the brand also focuses on fitness and health motivation. This was at a local beach close to my hometown!
